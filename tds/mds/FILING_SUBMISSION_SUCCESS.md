### Q: When will I get the Ack No?
**A:** Usually within 2-4 hours. The Tax Authority needs to receive and process your filing. You can check the filing status page periodically. It will update automatically once received.

### Q: Is my filing definitely submitted?
**A:** Yes! You got the confirmation message and can see it in the filing status as "SUBMITTED". The Filing ID `filing_demo_1765306863` is proof of submission.

### Q: What if the Ack No doesn't appear?
**A:**
1. Refresh the page (Ctrl+R)
2. Check back in an hour or two
3. Processing can take up to 24-48 hours
4. Your Filing ID confirms it was submitted

### Q: Can I submit Q2 as well?
**A:** Yes! Go to the filing status list, find your Q2 job, and if it shows "PENDING" status, you can click "Submit for E-Filing" again.

### Q: What's the difference between Filing ID and Ack No?
**A:**
- **Filing ID:** Proof YOU submitted it (filing_demo_1765306863)
- **Ack No:** Proof TAX AUTHORITY received it (will appear later)

### Q: Can I cancel the filing?
**A:** In demo mode, once submitted, you can't cancel. In production, you'd need to contact the Tax Authority.
