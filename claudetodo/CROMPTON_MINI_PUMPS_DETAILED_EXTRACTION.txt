SUMMARY NOTES:
===============================================================

1. All 15 mini pumps are 1 HP (0.75 kW) models
2. All are Single Phase (1PH) pumps
3. All are Self-Priming Regenerative design
4. Standard delivery pipe size is 25mm x 25mm
5. Regenerative pumps don't have traditional "stages" - they use a different impeller design
6. All pumps feature brass impellers and stainless steel inserts for corrosion resistance
7. Most have IP 55 protection and F-Class insulation
8. Typical lift capacity: 7.0 to 8.0 metres
9. Common features: Anti-jam winding, thermal overload protection, wide voltage design
10. Warranty: 1-2 years (varies by model)
11. ISI certification is likely for all (standard for Crompton residential pumps)
12. MNRE certification is not explicitly mentioned on product pages

PRICE RANGE: Rs. 4,525 (GLIDE PLUS II) to Rs. 13,050 (MINI MASTERPLUS I)

COLOR VARIANTS:
- Blue & Red (most models)
- Black & Silver (NILE DURA I, MASTER DURA I, CHAMP DURA I)
