Form Sections and Fields:

1. Customer Details:
   - Full Name (text, required)
   - Company / Organization Name (text)
   - Email Address (email, required)
   - Mobile Number (tel, required, +91 prefix hint)
   - Address / Location of Installation (textarea)
   - City (dropdown: Mumbai, Pune, Ahmedabad, Other)
   - Pin Code (number)
   - Preferred Contact Time (dropdown: Morning, Afternoon, Evening)

2. Application Details:

   - Purpose of Pump (text)
   - Installation Type (dropdown: Surface, Submersible, Booster, Dewatering, Openwell, Borewell)
   - Operating Medium (dropdown: Clean water, Muddy water, Sewage, Chemical, Hot water, Other)
   - Water Source (dropdown: Overhead tank, Underground tank, Borewell, River, Sump, Other)
   - Required Head (meters)
   - Required Discharge (LPM or mÂ³/hr)

   - Pipe Size (inches)

   - Operating Hours per Day (number)
   - Automation Needed (dropdown: Yes, No)
   - Existing Pump Model (text)
   - Upload Photos/Documents (file input, accept .jpg, .png, .pdf)

3. Product Preferences:
   - Preferred Brand (dropdown: Crompton, CG Power, Kirloskar, Open to suggestion)
   - Pump Type Interested In (multi-select or checkboxes: Centrifugal, Jet, Submersible, Monoblock, Borewell, Booster, Self-Priming, Others)
   - Material Preference (dropdown: Cast Iron, Stainless Steel, Bronze, Plastic, Open to suggestion)
   - Motor HP/kW (number)
   - Quantity Required (number)

4. Submission:
   - Consent Checkbox ('I authorize Bombay Engineering Syndicate to contact me regarding this inquiry.')
